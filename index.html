Tinkerhub Farook college is a campus community intiative of Tinkerhub foundation,a non-profit initiative aimed at making use of 21st-century technologies and learning methods to foster highly skilled young people empowered with technical and social skills Here we hold hands with Tinkerhub Foundation and take pride in learning and growing together with them.In this journey Tinkerhub Farook college focuses on enabling a peer to peer learning culture in the campus and promote a sense of community and belonging among the students
